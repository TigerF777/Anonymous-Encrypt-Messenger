🛡 Freedom Messenger

No surveillance. No spam. No control.

Created for those who value freedom of communication and privacy. No spam, no phone number link, no external access to your messages. Just you and your contact.

⚙ Features:

  • AES encryption on the client side (CryptoJS), without key transmission
  • End-to-end encryption for all data: messages, calls, media, files
  • Voice calls with real-time audio filters, each user’s filter is unique and heard by their contacts
  • Message replies, quotes, and deletion of individual messages and entire chats (including deleting from contact’s device)
  • File sending up to 10 GB without compression
  • Swipes on mobile: reply (right swipe), delete (left swipe) for messages; rename (right swipe), delete (left swipe) for chats
  • Context menu support on desktop (right-click)
  • Fullscreen viewing of images, GIFs, videos, and avatars
  • Link previews
  • "Typing" animation styled like the Matrix
  • Works as a PWA and supports Telegram WebView and mobile browsers
  • Crypto wallet integration (not limited to MetaMask), with easy adding and fast switching
  • Symmetric AES encryption key derived uniquely per chat ID — encryption and decryption happen client-side only
  • Server stores only encrypted data, never access to keys or plaintext

🧭 How to Start:

  • You have been assigned your public ID — share it with your chat partner
  • To start chatting, both participants must add each other's ID
  • Without exchanging IDs, the chat won't be created and messages won't be delivered
  • After exchanging IDs, an encrypted chat is created, accessible only to both of you
  • No one but you can read your messages
  • Enjoy encrypted calls, personalized voice filters, and large file sharing

🧠 Philosophy:

No middlemen. Just you, your contact, and a secure channel. No tracking, no junk. Absolute privacy and freedom of communication.
